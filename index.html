Publications | Service | Talks

I am a theoretical computer scientist. In particular, I am interested in understanding the inherent barriers, also known as "lower bounds", "impossibility results", or "separations" in different areas of theoretical computer science.

I got my PhD from Princeton in 2010 and joined Cornell as a postdoc. I joined the Computer Science Department of the University of Virginia in 2013 as an assistant professor, where I became an associate professor in 2019.
